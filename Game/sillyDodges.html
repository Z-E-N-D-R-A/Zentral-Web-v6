How to Play

Classic Mode

Move your blue dot to dodge incoming bullets (red dot).

Collect silly particles (green dot) to increase your score.

The higher the combo, the more points you get.

Game gets progressingly harder with faster and more bullets.


Survival Mode

Bullets (red dot) come in waves.

Survive each round by dodging the bullets.

More bullets spawn in every round.

Stay alive for as long as possible.


Controls

Mobile/Tablet: Drag or tap

Laptops/PC: Hovering using mouse
